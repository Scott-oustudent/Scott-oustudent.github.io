Hackathon Ranks and Pins

Hackathon Management Ranks and Pins

Admiral

Description for Admiral.

This role is the highest rank in the hackathon management. There will be no promotion to this role.

Vice Admiral

Description for Deputy Admiral.

This role is the second highest rank in the hackathon management. There will be no promotion to this role.

Chief Engineer

Description for Chief Engineer.

This role is responsible for overseeing the engineering team and ensuring the successful implementation of technical solutions. There will be no promotion to this role.

Communications Officer

Description for Communications Officer.

This role is responsible for managing all communications within the hackathon team and ensuring clear and effective information flow. There will be no promotion to this role.

Ambassador

Description for Ambassador.

This role is responsible for representing the hackathon team and building relationships with external stakeholders. There will be no promotion to this role.

Judge

Description for Judge.

This role is responsible for evaluating the hackathon projects and selecting the winners. There will be no promotion to this role.

Galactic Archives Participant Ranks and Pins

Cadet

Description for Cadet.

This role is the entry-level rank in the hackathon management.

Ensign

Description for Ensign.

This role is the second-level participant rank. This role will be awarded this title if the participant demonstrates exceptional skills and commitment in the hackathon. 20 available positions for this rank.

Lieutenant

Description for Lieutenant.

This role is the third-level participant rank. This role will be awarded this title if the participant demonstrates exceptional skills and commitment in the hackathon. Prerequisite knowledge of the hackathon tools and processes is required; the participant must have been awarded the Ensign rank. 10 available positions for this rank.

Lieutenant Commander

Description for Lieutenant Commander.

This role is the fourth-level participant rank. This role will be awarded this title if the participant demonstrates exceptional skills and commitment in the hackathon. Prerequisite knowledge of the hackathon tools and processes is required; the participant must have been awarded the Lieutenant rank. 5 available positions for this rank.

Commander

Description for Commander.

This role is the fifth-level participant rank. This role will be awarded this title if the participant demonstrates exceptional skills and commitment in the hackathon. Prerequisite knowledge of the hackathon tools and processes is required; the participant must have been awarded the Lieutenant Commander rank. 1 available position for this rank.


Mentors and Sponsors Ranks and Pins

Generic Mentor

Description for Generic Mentor.

This role is responsible for guiding and supporting participants throughout the hackathon. There will be no promotion to this role.

Subject Matter Expert

Description for Subject Matter Expert.

This role is responsible for providing expert knowledge and guidance in specific subject areas. There will be no promotion to this role.

Positional Sponsor

Description for Positional Sponsor.

This role is responsible for securing sponsorships and funding for the hackathon. There will be no promotion to this role.

Reputational Sponsor

Description for Reputational Sponsor.

This role is responsible for enhancing the reputation of the hackathon and its participants. There will be no promotion to this role.
